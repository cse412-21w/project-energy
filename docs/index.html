Washington State Available Solar Energy: This visualization shows suitability of using using the sun as a major energy source in Washington state. Major locations of energy consumption and average daily solar irradiation in kW*h/m^2 are shown to illustrate this. Eastern and Central WA are great candidates for a producing and benefiting from solar power, and (something about sending energy over to Western WA, where most energy is consumed, but its really cloudy) The plan is for an interactive image that shows higher resolution solar insolation data, population or energy consumption locations, and locations of all kinds of existing power plant.
